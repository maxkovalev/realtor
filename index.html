About

Meet Ella.

I'm Ella Lisitskova, and I've proudly called Boca Raton, Florida, home since 2007. My passion lies in the real estate world, where I specialize in residential and commercial properties, as well as rental properties in this vibrant South Florida community.

Being a Boca Raton resident myself, I bring a unique perspective to the table. I understand the ins and outs of this wonderful city, from its diverse neighborhoods to its exceptional amenities, schools, and the overall lifestyle it offers.

With my professional expertise and my personal bond with Boca Raton and surrounding areas, I'm your go-to resource for all things real estate in this beautiful city. Whether you're looking for your dream home, a savvy investment, or the perfect rental property, I'm here to make your real estate journey a success.

Let's find your place you call home in South Florida!
